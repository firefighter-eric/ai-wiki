# Alibaba Unveils Wan2.6 Series Enabling Everyone to Star in Videos

New visual generation models uplift cinematic and professional-quality video production for global creators.

Hangzhou, China, December 16, 2025 - Alibaba unveiled the latest evolution of its visual generation models, the `Wan2.6` series.

It enables creators to appear in AI-generated videos as themselves and in their own voices with flexible multi-shot storytelling, enhanced multi-person dialogue, and extended duration for richer narratives.

The `Wan2.6` series features a new reference-to-video generation model as well as comprehensive upgrades to its four existing models.

`Wan2.6-R2V` enables users to upload a character reference video with both appearance and voice, then use text prompts to generate new scenes starring that same character.

Users can create videos featuring a person, animal, object, or multiple subjects together while preserving the distinctive look and sound of the original reference.

Powered by multimodal reference generation capabilities, `Wan2.6-R2V` is described by Alibaba as China’s first reference-to-video generation model with consistent visuals and audio.

The series also includes enhancements to:

- `Wan2.6-T2V`
- `Wan2.6-I2V`
- `Wan2.6-image`
- `Wan2.6-T2I`

The new models introduce intelligent multi-shot storytelling capabilities that allow richer, more expressive narratives with visual consistency throughout.

Alibaba states that improved audio-visual synchronization and audio-to-video generation deliver more realistic scenes with richer sound effects.

Supporting video outputs of up to `15` seconds, the models give creators more room to develop their stories.

Combined with enhanced instruction-following precision and improved visual quality, the series is positioned for cinematic-style content with professional-grade results.

Users can access and deploy the models through `Model Studio` and the official `wan.video` website.
