Pricing

Each generation on the Vidu API platform uses credits. Credits are available for $0.005 each, with applicable sales tax based on your location. You can purchase credits as needed.
For high-volume orders, please contact us at： platform@vidu.studio

For details, please visit : Templates

Prompt Recommendation Features

Billing is based on the number of prompts returned. 10 credits will be deducted for every 5 prompts generated.

Lip Sync

Billing based on matching duration. Every 5 seconds, 20 credits will be cost.

Digital Human

If lip-sync or text to audio is not applied, only the video generation fee will be charged.

Video Extension & Multi Frame Generation

Note 1: Video extension currently supports adding 1–7 seconds. The credit consumption varies depending on the resolution and duration. For details, please refer to the Q2 Pricing Information.
Note 2: The pricing of Smart Multi-Frame generation depends on the resolution and duration of each segment. Every two input images form one segment.From the 2nd image to the (T–1)th image, each image serves as both the start and end frame.In total, a multi-frame video can contain up to 9 segments.The table below shows the pricing per single segment; the overall price depends on the total number of input images.
